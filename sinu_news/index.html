Siemens has been a pioneer in offering futuristic solutions for the ever increasing industrial demands. Adding to the list of advanced offerings, Siemens introduces the all new SINUMERIK controller PPU 161 for standard Turning and Milling machines. With an excellent cost to performance ratio, SINUMERIK controller PPU 161 is tailored to fit perfectly into standard machine segment.
This new addition to the SINUMERIK product range can support up to 5 axes/ spindle for both turning and milling applications. With an 8.4" display this new system offers high resolution (800x600 pixels), that ensures clear viewing and enables ease of use. The high speed-bus communication between CNC and drives ensures dynamic position control leading to high accuracy and optimum cutting performance.
With best in class features, this new solution comes along with digitally controlled SIMOTICS M-1PH1 spindle motors. Available with a power range of up to 15 kW and a speed range of maximum 10,000 rpm,these motors offer high overload and resolution capabilities enhancing the dynamic performance and accuracy of the machine tool. SIMOTICS S-1FL6 motors continue to be a favorite for the feed axes. Their design enables them to sustain in harsh environments in the standard machine market.
