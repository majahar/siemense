For date centers power must be continuously available and power quality must be impeccable in order to protect critical data. Data centers face several challenges: to minimize downtime, maximize energy efficiency, protect critical data / systems and support flexible growth. In recent times greater rack density for processing more power in the same amount of space places new demands on the power supply.
